Creating Argonne Accounts 
Go to https://accounts.mcs.anl.gov/index.php?tpl=request_identity.tpl
If you already have an ANL “Domain” account authenticate with it on the left panel, if not, fill out the right panel, accept the agreement, and click Continue
Fill out your personal information, and select the organization you’re affiliated with
Click next page
Fill in your username
Account class is Annual
Reason for requesting is “Participation in NCI data working group”
Account sponsor is “Maulik Shukla”
Set your password
Check MCS Forward – enter your email address
Click configure account email
Click request resources
Select Request or Remove Group Membership
Select the “nciftp” group
Click continue 
You will need to wait for your account to be approved
